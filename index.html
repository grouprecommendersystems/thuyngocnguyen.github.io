About Me

My name is Thuy-Ngoc Nguyen (call me Ngoc) and I'm from Vietnam. Currently, I'm a second year PhD student at Faculty of Computer Science, Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, Italy. My PhD research topic is "Conversational Group Recommender Systems", which aims at supporting the process of group decision-making. I'm interested in research activities on recommender systems, user modelling and human-computer interaction.
